Common Toppings on Carrot cake are:

  • carrot shavings
  • cream cheese frosting
  • cinnamon
  • nutmeg
  • pecans
  • walnuts
Layers of carrot cake with carrot shavings giving it an earthy flavor and cream cheese frosting in between giving it a creamy taste. Carrot cake is warm, spiced and comforting! Icing is made on carrot cake in the shape of carrots!
